#+BEGIN_VERSE
cold rain erasing snowfall
in such a season we made our ties
we never had stillness
of true winters
all moments turned in the wind
swept along waters
dropped sunlight from leaftips
the world held its breath
and we could not hold in time
the world breathed out
and i breathed you out in time
#+END_VERSE
