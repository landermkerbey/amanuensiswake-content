#+BEGIN_VERSE
these are the forms left me:
with cathedrals like forests, forests like cathedrals
with violent birth of light from dark on a Caravaggio
with words of a master that told our souls
with my feeble words, straining to capture your glow
with gentle fade of light glimpsed from a church-side alley
with lessons we bought dearly
with the memory of your lips upon mine
                        or my hands upon your face
                        soft and soft and soft
i will whisper to the part of you that has taken our shape
#+END_VERSE
